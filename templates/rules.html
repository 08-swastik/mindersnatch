RULES

Rule 1 Rule 2 Rule 3 Rule 4 Rule 5

Rule 1

Participants are to traverse a mission story line by choosing options at every point of the story. They will have to answer some subjective questions as well to ensure that they can move ahead.

Rule 2

The story is based on a mission to explore the bounds of time itself and your decisions determine where you go, and whether you live to see the light of the day.

Rule 3

For every node traversed you get a penalty of +1. This means the more number of times you reach dead ends and retry, the more penalties you accumulate. In case you reach the optimal node, you will be shown a page congratulating you for your successful survival. Least number of nodes should be taken to reach this.

Rule 4

You will be penalised based on the number of nodes you have traversed and the level you are on. For subjective questions, you get a penalty of +1 for solving it under 5 minutes and +2 for under 10 and +4 otherwise.

Rule 5
