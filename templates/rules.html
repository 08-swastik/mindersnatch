How you can save all of us!!

Rule 1

Participants are to traverse through the mission choosing one option for a particular situation.

Rule 2

In case you reach a dead node, you will be redirected to the 1st situation.

Rule 3

The situation could ask you to choose options, or it could be a subjective question to test your skills.

Rule 4

You will be penalised based on the number of nodes you have traversed and the level you are on. For subjective questions, you get a penalty of +1 for solving it under 5 minutes and +2 for under 10 and +4 otherwise.

Rule 5
